Biography

I am a PhD student advised by Dr. Alan Bovik in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UT Austin. I completed my Masters (Research) in 2018, where I worked with Dr. Rajiv Soundararajan in the Department of Electrical Communication Engineering at IISc Bangalore. I primarily work in the overlapping areas of image processing, computer vision and machine learning with a strong focus on developing quality assessment techniques for various image and video applications.
